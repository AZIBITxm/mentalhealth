Biography

Dr. Amelia Hayes is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 10 years of experience in the field. She holds a doctorate in Clinical Psychology from the University of California, Los Angeles, and has worked in various settings, including private practice, hospitals, and community mental health centers. Dr. Hayes is dedicated to providing compassionate and effective care to her clients, helping them navigate life's challenges and achieve their personal and emotional well-being goals.

Therapeutic Approach

Dr. Hayes utilizes an integrative approach to therapy, drawing from various evidence-based modalities such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and Mindfulness-Based Therapy. Her approach is tailored to meet the unique needs of each client, fostering a collaborative and supportive therapeutic relationship.
