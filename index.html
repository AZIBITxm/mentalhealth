About Dr. Amelia Carter

Dr. Carter is a licensed psychologist with over 10 years of experience. She holds a doctorate in clinical psychology and has worked in various settings. Her expertise includes cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based approaches, and interpersonal therapy. Dr. Carter is committed to providing the highest quality care.
